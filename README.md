# Baby-Assistant-App-
Baby AI Assistant tracking app that is designed to make parenting easy. 
Privacy Policy for Executive Parenting Dashboard
Effective Date: June 2, 2026
1. Introduction
Welcome to the Executive Parenting Dashboard ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your family. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our iOS and Android mobile applications.
Because the App is designed to help parents track sensitive newborn milestones, health data, and daily schedules, we have built the App with a "privacy-first, local-first" architecture.
2. Information We Collect
The App collects and processes information to provide you with an executive-level parenting management experience:
 Baby Tracking & Health Data: Information you manually input regarding feeding (bottle/breastfeeding), diaper changes, sleep durations, bath times, temperatures, and symptoms.
 Calendar Data: If you choose to link your Google Workspace, Microsoft Outlook, or Apple iCloud calendars, the App temporarily accesses your calendar events to identify scheduling conflicts and generate adaptive shift plans.
 AI Agent Prompts: Questions and data you submit to the AI Executive Agent for schedule resolution or pediatric advice.
3. How Data is Stored (Local-First)
Currently, we do not store your personal or family data on our own remote servers. All baby tracking data, health logs, and milestone progress are saved locally directly on your physical mobile device. If you delete the App without backing up your device, your data will be permanently deleted.
4. Third-Party Integrations & APIs
To provide advanced features, the App interacts with certain third-party services. These services are bound by their own privacy policies:
 Calendar Providers (Google, Microsoft, Apple): The App requests access to your calendars strictly to read event times and push adaptive schedule blocks. We do not copy, sell, or store your corporate calendar data on external databases.
 AI Processing (Google Gemini): To generate adaptive schedules and pediatric summaries, your AI chat queries and relevant tracking logs are securely sent to our AI provider. This data is used strictly to return a generated response to your device and is not used to train public AI models or sold to advertisers.
5. Health Data Usage (Apple App Store Compliance)
The App allows you to track health-related metrics (e.g., temperatures, feeding volumes).
 We do not use this health data for marketing or advertising purposes.
 We do not share this health data with third-party data brokers.
 This data is used solely to generate visual charts and PDF reports for your personal use and for sharing with your chosen pediatrician.
6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)
The App is designed for use by parents and caregivers over the age of 18. The App is not targeted at children, and we do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information directly from children under the age of 13.
7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as we add cloud-sync features or new integrations. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
